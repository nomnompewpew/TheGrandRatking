Anything Anytime Anyplace For No Reason At All...

-Frank Zappa

This is the mantra The Grand Ratking lives by - one that gives us the freedom to express any form of music within any context we choose. We are a collection of Boise musicians in pursuit of one of the most ridiculous side-projects fueled by our oddball chemistry.

Currently we have an epic song titled "Blood on Blood" and a music video for our Diablo III walkthrough titled "A Beginners Guide to Whimsyshire".

Coming this February we will be releasing our first EP titled "Sigil Magick" with five unreleased tracks recorded by Andy at the Chop Shop and Mastered by AudioSiege.
