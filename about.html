About Us...

Formed in 2017, The Grand Ratking is a 3-piece consisting of transplants from other bands looking for a project without boundaries, a direction or set genre. In this band anything goes - from doom to doo-wop, funk to punk, even some middle eastern pop and anything between.  

Our upcoming album "Sigil Magick" includes a diverse variety that showcases our very particular set of skills. Skills we have acquired over a very long career. Skills that make us a nightmare for people like you....wait no - Sigil Magick isn't that at all....

In this epic five track ride; the drummer plays guitar on two tracks, two songs include three part harmonies, almost every song has some sort of oddity with timing including a section in 15/4 and the bass parts tend take a lead like you may have never heard, with a full pedalboard.
